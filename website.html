Jiangxin Sun (孙江鑫)

I am a first year PhD student of the ELLIS PhD Program, supervised by Prof. Nicu Sebe in MHUG, and co-supervised by Prof. Daniel Cremers in TUM.

My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Machine Learning.Currently, my focus is on interpretable autonomous driving system, semantic/instance segmentation prediction and human motion prediction.

Prior to my PhD studies, I obtained my master's degree and bachelor's degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2023 and 2020, where I was supervised by Prof. Wei-Shi Zheng and Prof. Jian-Fang Hu. I was lucky to work closely with Prof. Wenjun Zeng and Prof. Jianguo Zhang.
